üéØ Course Objective

To enhance the skills to effectively shape organizational behaviour, implement successful change strategies, foster innovation, manage digital transitions, and build inclusive, adaptable, and sustainable work environments.

üß© Modules

MODULE 1: Organizational Culture Dynamics (8 Hours)

Organizational culture in shaping behavior and decision-making, Elements and Levels of Organizational Culture, Role of Leadership in Shaping Culture, Assessing Organizational Culture, Organizational Climate vs. Culture, Subcultures in Organizations, Culture as a Competitive Advantage, Types of organizational culture ‚Äì Clan, Adhocracy, Market, and Hierarchy. Digitalization - Impact of digitalization on organization culture. Theoretical frameworks ‚Äì Schein's model, Deal and Kennedy's model. Purpose-Driven Work Environments. Collaboration and Teamwork Enhancement.

MODULE 2: Change Management Models (10 Hours)

Overview of change management, Organizational change process, Challenges in organizational change, Forces Driving Change, Types of Organizational Change (Incremental vs. Transformational), Leadership Challenges in Change Management. Models ‚Äì Lewin's Change Management Model, Kotter's 8-Step Model, McKinsey 7S, ADKAR, Bridges' Transition Model. Competing Values Framework. Role of leadership and strategies for successful change implementation.

MODULE 3: Building Digital Culture (10 Hours)

Digital transformation and its impact on organizational culture, role of technology, challenges and opportunities of digital transformation. Strategies to strengthen culture in a digital environment, Cultural barriers to change, Impact of AI on organizational culture, Stakeholder engagement, Cross-functional collaboration, Cultural audits, Gap analysis, Mapping and benchmarking.

MODULE 4: Agile Change Management (8 Hours)

Adapting change management strategies to the digital era, Factors influencing change management, KPIs for change, Key challenges and successes, Leveraging technology, Addressing resistance to transformation, Ethical and digital change management, Agile frameworks, Crisis management, Institutionalizing change in culture.

MODULE 5: Trends and Practices (9 Hours)

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Belongingness (DEIB), Hybrid and Remote Work Models, Purpose-driven and Data-driven Culture, Innovation-driven Culture, Cross-Cultural Sensitivity, Agile Work Culture, Integration of Sustainability into Values and Practices, Automation, CSR, and Data-driven Decision Making.
